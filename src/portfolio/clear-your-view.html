Background

The rise in biomarker-targeted treatments for non-small cell lung cancer has prompted national guidelines promoting thorough biomarker testing. However, many community oncologists neglect to test for all suggested biomarkers, potentially compromising patient outcomes. We created an unbranded, data-driven web campaign called "Clear Your View" for Guardant Health to aid clinicians in recognizing the vital importance of biomarker testing in treatment choices.
